Smoking Policy : NO SMOKING PLEASE inside the salon (cigarettes, vapes, Iqos, electronic cigarettes, etc.)
For the comfort and well-being of all our clients and staff, we maintain a smoke-free environment within the salon. This includes all types of smoking devices, such as traditional cigarettes, vapes, Iqos, and electronic cigarettes. We appreciate your cooperation in ensuring a fresh and healthy atmosphere for everyone to enjoy.

Kids Policy : Your appointment is a time for yourself. We understand that sometimes children can be fussy, and we adore them! However, we do ask that you please leave infants, toddlers, and children under the age of 6 at home so that we can fully focus on you during your visit. This policy is in place to maintain a professional and serene atmosphere for all of our clients, ensuring that everyone can enjoy their treatments in a calm and relaxing environment. We understand that situations may arise, and if you need to reschedule due to your child's illness or lack of childcare, we will be happy to assist you.

Thank you for your cooperation and understanding. We look forward to providing you with an exceptional experience!
