about us

Our story began with a passion for beauty.

JT Beauty center stands for :

JT Beauty center is a salon for ladies who want to have beautiful nails , lashes , facials and body treatments.

It's a quiet , clean European-Arabic style beautique salon where the staff is trained by Josy (Belgian) . we use imported high quality products for your nails , lashes , body treatments and facials .

History

  • I started my career as cosmetology in Belgium in 1980
  • I teached cosmetology at a beauty Academy in Belgium
  • In 1989 I opened my first beauty salon in Belgium and did all beauty treatments myself
  • In 2000 I sold my business and got the honour to work for sothys (leader in Beauty treatments in more than 60 countries ) where i gave courses of their new products and treatments
  • In 2001 I made the big move to cairo and married my husband Tarek - We opened a salon in Maadi from that moment our business grew bit by bit and we hired more and more employees.
  • In 2008 we opened our second salon in New Cairo i continually train and retrain my staff to be able to keep a constant high standard of services in our salons.
  • My husband Tarek is now more involved and helps me managing both salons.
